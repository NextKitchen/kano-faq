## What is Kano?

Kano is an AI-powered website builder that helps brands create professional sites quickly.

- **Module-based** — Choose modules, edit content, publish in one click
- **AI-assisted** — From brand insight to content and visual design
- **Built for brands & F&B** — Fast setup, SEO automation, analytics
