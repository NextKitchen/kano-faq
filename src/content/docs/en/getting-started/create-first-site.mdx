## Steps

1. **Brand & tagline** — Name and slogan
2. **Service content** — Upload images and describe your services
3. **Visual design** — Cover image, logo, brand colors
4. **Custom buttons** — Links, CTA buttons, contact info

When done, AI generates the full site content; you can edit and publish right away.

## Drafts

- Auto-save every **2 seconds**
- Drafts are kept for **14 days** only
- You can resume from a draft
