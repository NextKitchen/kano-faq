## Ranking better on Google

- **Keywords** — Add [area] + [business type] in your tagline (e.g. "Best curry in Zhongshan")
- **Image ALT** — Fill in dish or product names for image search visibility
- **AI optimization** — Use the editor’s "AI optimize" for high-intent keywords

## High clicks but no conversions?

- Change "Contact us" to something like "Get today’s exclusive offer" or "Book without waiting"
- Use your **dark** brand color for buttons for better contrast
